Biography

Drew Branton (May 24th, 2001 - Present), was born at Sarasota Memorial Hospital in Sarasota, FL. He attended Little Disciples pre school, Lakeview Elementary School, Sarasota Christian for middle school, and high school at Cardinal Mooney Catholic high school. He played baseball throughout middle and high school, along with cross country and track his senior year. Drew applied to many different colleges, but ended up deciding on Florida Southern College in Lakeland, FL to stay close to home. Drew is a Finance major and has a minor in Computer Science. He plans on graduating with his undergraduate degree in December of 2023, afterwards he is going to pursue his MBA in Supply Chain Management with FSC as well. He will be working this summer as an Area Manager Intern for Amazon in their opsuniversity program. He will be in Canton, Mississippi, where he will be working at a state of the art, 3 million square foot robotics fullfillment center that was recently opened in 2022.
